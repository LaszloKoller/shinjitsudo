About Us

Shinjitsu Dō, which means "The Way of Integrity" (or The Way of Truth), is a martial arts association that was founded in 1999 in order to provide a safe and friendly atmosphere for training in Isshin-ryū Karate.

If you have any questions about Shinjitsu Dō Kai (as our association is more formally known), please do not hesitate to contact us.
